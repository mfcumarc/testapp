Enjoy the show with 10,000 of your closest friends

Moozedoo is an experience. It’s about connecting with other people who enjoy music and like to express their individuality. Interaction is what makes us all human - experience the humanity, share the fun.

- Experience -

Chill-out Tent

Get away from the action in the chill-out tent. A soothing mist keeps you nice and cool and there are chairs and nice rugs to sit on.

Food Truck Rally

Get your grub on at the food truck rally. Over a dozen different trucks are going to be there and there are vegan/vegetarian options.

Camping

Camping spots are available on a first-come first-served basis for the weekend. Tents only, no campers. Camping is rustic.

Skate Ramp

Shred it up on our community skate park. Conveniently located right next to the first aid tent! Totally rad!
